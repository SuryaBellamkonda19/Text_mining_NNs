# Sentiment Analyzer - User Guide

Welcome to the **Sentiment Analyzer** app! This tool predicts the sentiment (**Positive**, **Negative**, **Neutral**) of any sentence or tweet.

---

## How to Use:
1. Enter your text in the input box below.
2. Click **Predict Sentiment**.
3. See the predicted sentiment displayed with a **color-coded indicator** and **emoji**.
